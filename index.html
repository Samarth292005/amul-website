Common Questions

What are the main ingredients? expand_more
Amul Kool Rose consists of high-quality standardized milk, sugar, and natural identical flavoring substances (Rose). It is sterilized for safety and shelf stability.
How should I store it? expand_more
It can be stored in a cool and dry place. However, for the best experience, we recommend serving it chilled.
What is the shelf life? expand_more
Thanks to our advanced sterilization process, Amul Kool Rose has a shelf life of up to 6 months from the date of manufacture when stored properly.
